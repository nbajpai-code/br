# Warren Buffett's Book Recommendations

Warren Buffett, the "Oracle of Omaha," is famous for his investment wisdom. His recommendations are essential reading for anyone interested in finance and business.

## Investing & Business Classics
*   **The Intelligent Investor** by Benjamin Graham (Buffett's top recommendation)
*   **Security Analysis** by Benjamin Graham and David L. Dodd
*   **Business Adventures: Twelve Classic Tales from the World of Wall Street** by John Brooks
*   **Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits** by Philip A. Fisher
*   **Poor Charlie's Almanack** by Charles T. Munger
*   **The Outsiders: Eight Unconventional CEOs and Their Radically Rational Blueprint for Success** by William Thorndike Jr.

## Other Recommendations
*   **The Great Crash of 1929** by John Kenneth Galbraith
*   **Shoe Dog** by Phil Knight
*   **The Essays of Warren Buffett** by Lawrence A. Cunningham
*   **The Most Important Thing** by Howard Marks
*   **Where Are the Customers' Yachts?** by Fred Schwed Jr.
*   **The Little Book of Common Sense Investing** by John C. Bogle
