# Sam Altman's Book Recommendations

Sam Altman, CEO of OpenAI and former president of Y Combinator, recommends books that span technology, business strategy, and philosophy.

## Technology & Business
*   **Zero to One** by Peter Thiel and Blake Masters
*   **Superintelligence: Paths, Dangers, Strategies** by Nick Bostrom
*   **Blitzscaling** by Reid Hoffman and Chris Yeh
*   **Winning** by Jack Welch and Suzy Welch
*   **The Score Takes Care of Itself** by Bill Walsh
*   **Dealers of Lightning** by Michael A. Hiltzik
*   **The Making of a Manager** by Julie Zhuo
*   **Secrets of Sand Hill Road** by Scott Kupor

## Philosophy, Science & Society
*   **The Beginning of Infinity** by David Deutsch (Cited as a favorite)
*   **Man's Search for Meaning** by Viktor Frankl
*   **Thinking, Fast and Slow** by Daniel Kahneman
*   **Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind** by Yuval Noah Harari
*   **The Death and Life of Great American Cities** by Jane Jacobs
*   **Meditations** by Marcus Aurelius

## Fiction & Literature
*   **Brave New World** by Aldous Huxley
*   **Anna Karenina** by Leo Tolstoy
*   **The Republic** by Plato
*   **Endurance: Shackleton's Incredible Voyage** by Alfred Lansing
