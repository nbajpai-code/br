# Satya Nadella's Book Recommendations

Satya Nadella, CEO of Microsoft, emphasizes a "growth mindset" and continuous learning. His list reflects a blend of leadership, technology, and social science.

## Leadership & Mindset
*   **Mindset: The New Psychology of Success** by Carol S. Dweck (A cornerstone of his leadership philosophy)
*   **Nonviolent Communication: A Language of Life** by Marshall B. Rosenberg
*   **Hit Refresh** by Satya Nadella (His own book on transforming Microsoft)
*   **The Ride of a Lifetime** by Robert Iger
*   **The Road to Character** by David Brooks
*   **Forged in Crisis** by Nancy Koehn

## Technology & Society
*   **The Great Convergence** by Richard Baldwin
*   **The Narrow Corridor** by Daron Acemoglu and James A. Robinson
*   **The Innovator's Dilemma** by Clayton M. Christensen
*   **Deep Learning** by Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio, and Aaron Courville
*   **AI Superpowers** by Kai-Fu Lee
*   **The Rise and Fall of American Growth** by Robert J. Gordon
*   **Prosperity** by Colin Mayer
*   **Thinking, Fast and Slow** by Daniel Kahneman
